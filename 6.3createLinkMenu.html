Really Honest Book Reviews

I read a lot of books about many different subjects. Though I'm not a book critic, and I don't do this for a living, I enjoy a really good read every now and then. Here's a list of books that I've read recently:

  • The Rainbow Returns by E. Smith A fantasy story set in biblical times. Slow at times, but interesting.
  • How to be a Great Programmer by J. Hacker A must-read for anyone who wants to be a programmer. It's a bit dry, but very informative.
  • Mastering the Internet by A. Webhead A great book for anyone who wants to learn about the Internet. It's a bit outdated, but still useful.
  • The Silly Person's Guide to Seriousness by M. Nott A funny book that makes you think. It's a bit silly, but very entertaining.
